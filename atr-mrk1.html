ATR Mrk. 1

Where it Started.

We wanted to design a robot to climb on a alternative terrain that neither of us ahd worked on before. We narrowed our choices between Vertical Steel (with electro-magnents) or Veritcal Glass (with suction cups). We decided on the Glass.

Initial Ideas and Prototypes.

Our first step was to research suction cups. We found to promising systems. We ordered an electronic assembely line suction cup and also a DIY suction, tube, and pump system. After testing we decided on the DIY version dut to the fact that the other required that the suction be completely flat on teh surface to engage suction force.
