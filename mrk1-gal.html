Laser Cut Bar

This part for our chassis was laser cut out of four pieces and then super glued together.

Bottom Bar Suction Cup Holder

This is attached to the bottom bar and holds a suction cup. It is able to articulate to line up with the angle of the window.

3D Printed Gear

This 3D printed Gear is apart of our Gearbox Assembely. It has a small gear attached to a larger Gear. A couple of the together help to make our ideal gear ratio.

Chassis Bar

This is he chassis bar that we use to hold everything together. This is it before we designed it to be laser cut.

Rack and Pinnion

This was the first idea for an extending arm. We laser cut this Rack and Pinnion and tested it alongside our other idea, the forebar. We decided to go with the Forebar

Top Bar

This is the 3D printed piece that connects to the top of our Forebar. It Holds one of our suction cups and an adjusting servo for that suction cup.
